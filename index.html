The Landscape of Financial Advice Today

Exploring how financial advice has changed over time and the concurrent decline in financial literacy.

Key Insights:

  • The volume of financial advice search terms has increased exponentially over the past two decades.
  • While financial advice has become more abundant, financial literacy has declined across generations.
  • The most searched financial terms have shifted from traditional banking concepts to more speculative investments.
  • Gen Z shows the lowest financial literacy scores despite having the greatest access to financial information.
  • The rise of social media as a source of financial advice correlates with decreased financial literacy.
